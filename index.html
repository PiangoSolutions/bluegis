🔭 What is BlueGIS?

Problem Solved

Reactive management & fragmented data

Ports and offshore wind farms invest millions in blind maintenance. Satellite data exists but is complex and requires scarce, expensive experts.

Our Solution

Autonomous AI Agent

A multi-agent system that monitors millimetric deformations, fuses oceanographic data, and generates executive reports with actionable recommendations.

Key Differentiator

Decisions, not data

While others sell maps, we deliver answers: "Where to invest? Which asset is at real risk? How to avoid environmental fines?"

🎯 Specialized services by stakeholder

⚓ 1. Port Authorities

Deformation monitoring of docks and dikes, early flood alerts from subsidence + sea level rise, and predictive maintenance planning.

💨 2. Marine Energy Operators

Offshore foundation stability, seabed analysis for submarine cables, and cable route optimization.

🐟 3. Aquaculture & Coastal Fisheries

Algal bloom alerts, water quality monitoring, and risk assessment for concession areas.

🏖️ 4. Coastal Tourism & Urban Resilience

Shoreline evolution tracking, beach erosion analysis, and climate adaptation planning for coastal cities.

🏛️ 5. Regulators & Environmental Agencies

Automated environmental surveillance, change detection for unauthorized works, and SDG-aligned compliance reports.

🔬 6. Research Institutions

Harmonized time series for scientific validation, access to processed InSAR data, and collaborative R&D projects.

🧠 Innovation: AI Agents + InSAR + Copernicus

🛰️ Multi-satellite fusion

Integration of Sentinel-1 (InSAR), Sentinel-3 (oceanic), Sentinel-6 (sea level), plus CMEMS, C3S and CLMS services.

🤖 Multi-agent orchestrator

LangGraph + DeepSeek self-hosted. Fine-tuning capability with proprietary geospatial data. 100% data sovereignty.

📊 From data to decision

Executive recommendations in Event – Risk – Plan format, aligned with ISO 31000 and the Disaster Risk Reduction strategy.
